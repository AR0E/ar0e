## Small systems note

Consider the one-pole low-pass response

$$
H(s) = \frac{1}{1 + s/\omega_c}.
$$

For $\omega \ll \omega_c$, the gain is approximately $1$. For $\omega \gg \omega_c$, the magnitude decays like $1/\omega$.
